Yejoka Gardens brings the authentic flavors of Ethiopia to Nairobi in a serene garden setting.

  • Traditional Ethiopian cuisine with authentic recipes
  • Tranquil garden atmosphere perfect for relaxation
  • Private dining areas for special occasions
  • Wide selection of beers, cocktails, and traditional Ethiopian coffee

Located in the heart of Kilimani, Yejoka Gardens offers an escape from the city bustle with its lush greenery and romantic ambiance. Our menu features traditional Ethiopian dishes prepared with authentic spices and served with injera, the traditional Ethiopian flatbread.

Why Choose Yejoka Gardens

Yejoka Gardens stands out as Nairobi's premier Ethiopian dining experience, combining authentic cuisine with a unique garden atmosphere that transports you to Ethiopia.
